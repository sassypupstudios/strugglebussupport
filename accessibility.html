Accessibility

Struggle Bus Support is intended to be usable by people with disabilities, people using assistive technology, people on mobile devices, and people who need information quickly.

We aim to support readable text, keyboard navigation, clear labels, skip links, plain language, and simple page structure.

If something is difficult to read, navigate, understand, or use with assistive technology, please email info@strugglebussupport.org.
