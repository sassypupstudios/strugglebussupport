Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Struggle Bus Support is operated by Sassy Pup Studios LLC as a free community resource directory for Spartanburg County, South Carolina.

You do not need an account, login, payment, or personal profile to browse the directory.

If you submit a resource suggestion or correction, we may receive the information you choose to provide, including resource details and optional submitter contact information. Optional contact information is used only to review or follow up about the submission.

Basic technical information may be collected by hosting, security, analytics, and advertising services. This may include device information, browser information, pages visited, referring pages, approximate location, cookies, similar identifiers, and security logs.

This site may display ads to help cover operating costs. Advertising partners, including Google, may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar identifiers to serve, measure, secure, limit, or personalize ads depending on your settings and applicable law.

The directory is not intended for confidential records or private case details. Please do not submit highly sensitive information through this site.

Resource listings may link to outside websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, accuracy, or availability of outside websites.

We want the site to be usable by people with disabilities and people using assistive technology. Privacy or accessibility questions may be sent to info@strugglebussupport.org.
