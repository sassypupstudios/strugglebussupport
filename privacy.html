Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Struggle Bus Support ("we," "our," or "the site") is a free community resource directory. This policy explains what information is collected when you use this website, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or log in. We do not knowingly collect personal information from site visitors who are only browsing or searching for resources.

Suggestion Form. If you submit a resource suggestion or correction through our Suggest a Resource form, you may optionally provide your name and email address along with information about the resource. This information is transmitted securely and stored in our private Airtable database so we can follow up and credit your submission. We do not sell or share this information with third parties. We use it only to maintain the directory.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) automatically collects standard server log data when you visit any page. This may include your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and the pages you visit. This data is used for security and performance purposes and is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy. We do not use this data to identify or track individual visitors.

3. Cookies

We do not set our own tracking cookies. However, third-party services embedded in or accessed through this site may set cookies on your device — see Section 4 for details.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some third-party features but will not affect your ability to search for resources on this site.

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense (Advertising)

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. These cookies help Google deliver ads that are relevant to you.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or aboutads.info. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Airtable

Resource suggestion submissions are processed and stored using Airtable, a cloud-based database service. Only information you choose to submit in the form is sent to Airtable.

5. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our form, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Links to External Sites

This site contains links to third-party websites (resource organization websites, Google Maps, phone dial links, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@strugglebussupport.org or use the Suggest a Resource form.
